Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an introductory offer?
A: No. This is not an introductory or promotional rate. It is month-to-month pricing with no long-term contracts and you can cancel at anytime.

What is pay-as-you go?
A: It's a simple way to pay for your CenturyLink Internet service. Your credit or debit card will be charged automatically each month. No credit check.

What if I decide to add other services?
A: Great! You can add home phone or tv services at any time and still get your Internet on a pay-as-you go basis.
